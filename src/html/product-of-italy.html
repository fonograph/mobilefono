A premium cookbook and cooking show for iPad

    productofitaly.ca

    With Orange Paperclip

    Role: Technical Lead

    Product of Italy was an ambitious attempt to create a single iPad app containing an interactive cookbook, a cooking show, and a digital kitchen helper.

    The final product contains over 100 recipes and 15 episodes divided into a series of "supper clubs" available as in-app purchases. Features include bookmarking and note-taking within episodes, a grocery list builder, a step-by-step interactive guide for each recipe, the ability to make notes on individual recipe steps, and more.

    Winner, Best Cooking App, 2014 Best App Ever Awards
