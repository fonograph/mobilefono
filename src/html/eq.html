Gamified concussion management for young athletes.

    highmark.tech

    With Highmark Interactive

    Role: Lead Front-End Developer

    EQ contains six different neurological tests, all clinically tested and sensitive to symptoms of concussion, anxiety, lack of sleep, and more. In the event of a worrisome hit or injury, a post-collision test can be run to assist in deciding what to do next. We adapted the tests into engaging games to test the brain, and and built rewards for consistent play like new characters, outfits, and funny fans to cheer users on.
