A physical game with digital controllers, for three to infinite players.

    Independent

    You know that scene in Kill Bill where Uma Thurman takes on the Crazy 88 in an epic swordfight? This game is that scene. One swordmaster takes on a legion of nameless attackers, complete with music, sound cues, and flashing lights. It was built with foam swords, Playstation Move controllers, magnets, and a lot of velcro.

    Legion has been exhibited at Harbourfront Beatz Festival, Maker Festival TO, and the CNE Gaming Garage.
