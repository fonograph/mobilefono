A pair of mobile-first HTML5 games based on the TV show

    ytv.com/snowboard-challenge / ytv.com/science-challenge

    With Stitch Media

    Role: Technical Director / Co-Game Designer

    Stitch Media was tasked with creating accessible but engrossing games for a young audience, to coincide with the launch of the Max & Shred TV show. We created two: Snowboard Challenge is a side-scrolling endless runner, where the player scores by hitting ramps and doing a variety of snowboard tricks in the air. Science Rush is a tower defense game based on the Plants vs Zombies model, where the player deploys a variety of high tech distractions to stop obnoxious teenagers.

    The games were developed in pure Javascript, using the Phaser and Pixi.js frameworks.
