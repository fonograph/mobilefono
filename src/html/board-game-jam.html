Seven years of community driven board-game-building fun.

    With Adam Clare and a team of volunteers

    Role: Co-organizer

    Board Game Jam was an annual weekend-long ticketed event that ran from 2011 to 2017. Each year, teams of participants spent two days brainstorming, designing and prototyping their own original games. We provided the physical supplies, along with guidance and advice from some board game professionals.

    Mont Trottoir has been played in Toronto, NYC, Pittsburgh, Berlin, Melbourne and Perth. The full rules and materials are available here.
