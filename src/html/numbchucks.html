A suite of web games based on the TV show

    Hyper Street Dash / Kung-Fu Bowling / Fists of Punching

    With 9 Story

    Role: Lead Developer / Co-Game Designer

    9 Story was tasked with creating accessible but engrossing games for a young audience, to coincide with the launch of the Numb Chucks TV show. We created three: Hyper Street Dash is a side-scrolling endless runner, where the player can smash through a variety of objects using kung-fu moves. Epic Bowling effectively a top-down driving game, where the player steers a ball to hit pins and power ups along the course. And Fists of Punching is a retro homage to side-scrolling beat-em-ups in the vein of Streets of Rage.

    The games were developed in Flash / AS3.
