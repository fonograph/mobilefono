The world's first two-player hipster clown performance/argument simulator.
    Starring Morro and Jasp, Canadian Comedy Award and Dora Award winners.

    game.morroandjasp.com

    With U.N.I.T. Productions

    Role: Lead Designer & Developer

    Morro & Jasp: Unscripted was a collaboration the titular clown duo, a well-known act in the Canadian theatre world, who have created 10 shows (and a cookbook) over more than a decade of work.

    Using a separate tablet or phone, each player takes the role of either Morro or Jasp, a pair of clown sisters putting on a show they’re entirely unprepared for. The performance unfolds with each player choosing each and every line for their clown. With over 25,000 words of dialogue and 50 different endings, players have the opportunity to create a great show or a terrible one, through fights, reconciliations, romantic comedy, Shakespeare, drunkenness, Vaudeville acts, staring matches, lion attacks, and so on. Every playthrough is like co-creating a live performance, and since each one lasts only a few minutes, there’s ample opportunity to play together in a myriad of different ways.
