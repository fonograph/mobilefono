A digital collectable card game — Hearthstone meets Hieronymus Bosch

    rivalbooksofaster.com

    With Stitch Media

    Role: Technical Director / Co-Game Designer

    Rival Books of Aster is an iPad game in the popular CCG genre. It distinguishes itself through a unique combo mechanic, a very unconventional grimoire-based aesthetic developed by fine artists, and an aspect of collaborative storytelling. Each of the 200 "spells" has a unique story told within an in-game chronicles, and these stories continually unfold and expand, as influenced by the collective gameplay actions of all players over time.
