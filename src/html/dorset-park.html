A workshop series for making site-specific games with kids.

    handeyesociety.com/dorset-park

    With Hand Eye Society

    Role: Project Coordinator

    In July 2016, Hand Eye Society ran a series of outdoor weekend workshops in Dorset Park, Scarborough. Fourteen kids worked with a group of game artists to learn about game design, and ultimately design their own games, which were then written up on the project website.
