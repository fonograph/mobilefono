A physical game for sidewalks, played internationally

    Independent

    Mont Trottoir is a "site-specific-ish" game designed to take advantage of unique architecture in urban sidewalks and pedestrian spaces. Using turn-based parkour-style movements, teams of players compete to complete a course, imagined as climbing a mountain. To avoid "falling", they need to stay off the ground, or hold on to each other as one players clings to safe ground.

    Mont Trottoir has been played in Toronto, NYC, Pittsburgh, Berlin, Melbourne and Perth. The full rules and materials are available here.
