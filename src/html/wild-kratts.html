A suite of 10+ browser games and activities teaching about wildlife

    pbskids.org/wildkratts

    With Chocolate Liberation Front

    Role: Technical Lead / Co-Game Designer

    More than a simple series of mini-games, Wild Kratts was a range of diverse and integrated activities. This included 6 games from diverse genres, including a cheetah racing game, a kangaroo fighting game, a firefly rhythm game, and more. Completing games allowed players to unlock "creature power suits", which could be used in 5 different habitats -- explorable side-scrolling environments featuring natural wonders to be discovered by utilizing the collected powers. Finally, an avatar builder allowed players to intricately customize the avatar that they controlled within the habitats, right down to individual facial details.

    Winner, Best Companion Website, 2012 Kidscreen Awards
